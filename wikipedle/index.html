A Wikipedia game inspired by Wordle


You get 6 tries to guess the title of the wikipedia article.


The First Clue 📚 is the section titles of the article.

The Second Clue 📰 is the first 5 sentences from the article with
important words redacted.

The Third Clue 👀 is a brief description of the article.

Then 3 additional paragraphs 🤦 will be added until the article is guessed or there
are no more paragraphs.
